Before your blood donation

  • Eat a healty meal before your donation . Preferably green leafy vegetables
  • Get plenty of sleep the night before you plan to donate . Atleast 8hr the night before
  • Drink plenty of water before the donation . About 3500ml the day before
  • Avoid drinking alcohol the day prior to blood donation

After your blood donation

  • Stay in refreshments area for atleast 20mins
  • Eat food rich in vitamin C like , green leafy vegetables, oranges, lemon etc
  • Drink pleanty of water , at least 3 good sized water in first 3 hours
  • Eat a light but fulfilling meal after donating blood
